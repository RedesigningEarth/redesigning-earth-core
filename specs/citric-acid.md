# Citric Acid — Material Specification (Extended)

## Chemical identity
- Formula: C6H8O7
- CAS: 77-92-9

## Physical & thermal properties

| Property | Typical value | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Density | ~1.66 g/cm³ | Solid |
| Melting / decomposition | ~153 °C | Decomposes |
| Solubility | Very high (water) | Polar |
| Acidity (pKa1) | ~3.1 | Acidic |

## Functional role
- Hydrogen bond donor
- DES component

## Mechanical relevance
- Not load-bearing
- Alters bonding environment

## RE relevance
- Bond modifier
- Excess causes brittleness
