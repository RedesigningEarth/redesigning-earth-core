# Choline Chloride — Material Specification (Extended)

## Chemical identity
- Formula: C5H14ClNO
- CAS: 67-48-1

## Physical & thermal properties

| Property | Typical value | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Density | ~1.2 g/cm³ | Solid |
| Melting / decomposition | ~302 °C | Decomposes |
| Hygroscopicity | High | Moisture uptake |
| Solubility | Very high (water) | Polar systems |

## Functional role
- Hydrogen bond acceptor
- DES component

## Mechanical relevance
- Non-structural
- Enables viscous phase formation

## RE relevance
- Partial plasticization aid
- Introduces solvent-like behavior
